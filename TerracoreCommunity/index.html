About the Regions

Equenor East Island is divided into two main regions: Celestial and Terraform, each with its own unique terrain, climate, and challenges.

Celestial Region – A lush and vibrant landscape filled with dense forests, shimmering lakes, and majestic peaks. Key areas include the Celestial Forest, Eye’s Lake, and Mount Celestial. This region is ideal for exploration, resource gathering, and discovering ancient secrets.

Terraform Region – A harsher, more extreme environment with arid deltas, larger beach, and rugged terrain. It’s home to Terraform Delta, Terraform Cylen, and Terraform Elvyn. Rich in valuable minerals but dangerous due to unpredictable weather and unstable landscapes.

Whether you seek the beauty of the Celestial side or the raw challenge of the Terraform lands, each area offers unique adventures and opportunities for brave explorers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are some of the most common questions we get from the community. Click on a question to reveal the answer.

What is Terracore?

Terracore is a Roblox exploration and social hangout game set on an undefined planet filled with stunning landscapes, hidden secrets, and unique challenges.

How can I play?

You can play by clicking the “Play Now” button above or visiting our Roblox game page directly.

Is Terracore free?

Yes! Terracore is completely free to play. However, some cosmetic items and special perks may require Robux.

How do I rank up?

You rank up by increasing your playtime and completing in-game activities. Each rank unlocks new titles and recognition in the community.

Where can I get updates?

Follow us on our social media channels listed at the bottom of this page to get the latest news, events, and update announcements.

Do I need special hardware to play?

Depends. If your device can run Roblox smoothly, you can enjoy Terracore without any special requirements.

Can I play with friends?

Absolutely! You can invite your friends to join the same server and explore together.

Are there events in Terracore?

Yes. We host seasonal events and community challenges with unique rewards and badges.

How do I report a bug or issue?

You can report bugs by sending feedback through the Feedback page or contacting us on Discord.

How can I become a moderator?

We occasionally recruit new moderators from our active and trusted community members. Stay engaged and follow updates for announcements.

📜 Moderator Eligibility Rules

  • ✅ Must be an active player for at least 3 months.
  • ✅ Maintain a positive and respectful attitude towards all players.
  • ✅ Have no major rule violations or bans in the past 6 months.
  • ✅ Must be at least 16 years old.
  • ✅ Be able to communicate clearly and resolve conflicts fairly.
  • ✅ Actively contribute to the Terracore community (events, support, reporting issues).
